Honors & Rewards

President's List

The President's List celebrates students who have taken a minimum of 12 credit hours during the semester and attained a perfect 4.0 semester Grade Point Average. Being placed on the this list recognizes that the student is dedicated to attaining their academic goals and willing to go the extra mile to achieve them. This honor is noted on the student's academic transcript and becomes a permanent part of the student's official record at Florida Gulf Coast University.
